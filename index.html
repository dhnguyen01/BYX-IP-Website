About BYX Island Party

An annual music festival hosted by Beta Upsilon Chi to bring people together and support charitable causes.

Our Mission

BYX Island Party aims to create an unforgettable experience that not only entertains but also makes a positive impact on our community by supporting local and national charities.

Event Highlights

  • Live performances by top artists
  • Food trucks and vendors
  • Interactive games and activities
  • Opportunities to connect and network
